Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EKT Training (“we”, “us”), operated by Elijah King Turner, collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use this website and book training sessions. We aim to follow the principles set out in the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the CPRA (CCPA/CPRA), and Canada’s PIPEDA where they apply to you.

This document is a good-faith, plain-language template. Privacy law depends on where you and your clients live and how the business actually operates. Please have it reviewed by a qualified attorney before relying on it. Authoritative starting points include the FTC’s guidance at ftc.gov, the California Attorney General’s CCPA pages at oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa, and the UK ICO at ico.org.uk.

What we collect

  • Account information — your name, email address, and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in readable form.
  • Booking information — the training time slots you reserve.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our payment processor, Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card number. We keep only a Stripe customer reference and your subscription status.
  • Messages — anything you send us through the contact form.
  • Technical data — a session cookie needed to keep you signed in, and standard server logs (such as IP address) used for security and to prevent abuse.

Why we use it (legal bases)

  • To create and operate your account and let you book sessions — to perform our contract with you.
  • To take payment for memberships — to perform our contract with you.
  • To secure the site, prevent fraud, and respond to enquiries — our legitimate interests.
  • Where required, with your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

Cookies

We use a single, strictly-necessary cookie to keep you signed in and a security token cookie to protect forms against cross-site request forgery. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. Because these cookies are essential to the service you request, they are exempt from consent under the ePrivacy Directive.

Who we share it with

We share the minimum necessary data with service providers who help us run the site: Stripe (payment processing — see stripe.com/privacy) and our email delivery provider for contact messages. We do not sell or “share” your personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

How long we keep it

We keep account and booking data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations. You can delete your account at any time (see below).

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a portable copy of your personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California residents additionally have the right to know, delete, correct, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

  • Access & portability: from your booking account you can download all the data we hold about you as a machine-readable JSON file at any time.
  • Erasure: you can permanently delete your account and bookings from the account panel.
  • Other requests: contact us using the details below and we will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

How we protect it

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), password hashing with bcrypt, cross-site request forgery protection, a strict Content-Security-Policy, rate limiting on sign-in, and secure, signed session cookies. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and to limit what we collect in the first place.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data without verifiable parental consent.

Contact

For any privacy request or question, contact Elijah King Turner at elijah02.ek@gmail.com or through our contact page.
